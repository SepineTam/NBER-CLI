NBER · W33942

Global Trade Reallocation and the New Geography of Production

Laura Alfaro · Pol Antràs · Davin Chor

Abstract

We study how firms reorganize production networks when trade costs become more uncertain. Using newly linked transaction and customs records, we document a shift from cost-minimizing supply chains toward regionally diversified production. The adjustment is gradual, uneven across industries, and concentrated among firms with prior international experience.
